HERTZ CAR RENTAL - TERMS AND CONDITIONS - SPAIN (ES)

THIRD PARTY LIABILITY
Third party liability insurance is provided in accordance with applicable Spanish legislation
and the statutory minimum requirements as determined by Spanish law.

GRACE PERIOD
Reservations not collected within 29 minutes of the scheduled pickup time will be 
automatically cancelled with no refund applicable.

DRIVING LICENCE REQUIREMENTS  
A valid driving licence held for a minimum of 1 year is required. Licences issued outside 
the EU/EEA must be accompanied by an International Driving Permit. Digital licences and 
photocopies will not be accepted.

PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS
A valid credit card in the main driver's name is required for the security deposit.
Debit cards, prepaid cards, and cash are not accepted for the deposit.
The credit card must have sufficient funds for the full deposit amount.

CROSS-BORDER CONDITIONS
Cross-border travel outside Spain requires prior written authorisation from Hertz.
Travel to Morocco, Albania, Kosovo, Belarus, and Russia is strictly prohibited.
A Green Card (International Motor Insurance Certificate) is required for approved 
cross-border travel within Europe.
